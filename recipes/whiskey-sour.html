Whiskey Sour

Description

A delightfully refreshing cocktail with bright lemon notes and a sultry bourbon background. Serve over ice and enjoy as the start to an evening.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 1 oz Fresh Lemon Juice (squeeze yourself)
  • .75 oz Simple Syrup (1:1 ratio)
  • 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker tin and fill with ice
  2. Shake for 20 seconds or until properly diluted
  3. Double strain through a tea strainer into a rocks glass over ice
  4. Garnish with orange slice and cherry and serve
