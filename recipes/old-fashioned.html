Old Fashioned

Description

A boozy and classic old fashioned to satisfy anyone's craving for bourbon or a strong sipper.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 oz Bourbon (Preferably bonded, such as Old Grandad Bonded)
  • .25 oz Rich Simple Syrup (2:1 ratio)
  • 2 Dashes Angostura
  • Orange Peel

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients except orange peel in stirring glass
  2. Fill with ice and stir for 25 seconds, or until properly diluted
  3. Strain into rocks glass over ice
  4. Garnish with expressed Orange Peel and serve
