Sazerac

Description

A classic New Orleans cocktail, the Sazerac is the pinnacle of sippers.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Rye Whiskey
  • .25 oz Rich Simple Syrup (2:1 ratio)
  • 4-6 Dashes Peychaud's Bitters
  • Lemon Peel
  • 1 barspoon Absinthe (for rinsing)

Preparation

  1. Chill rocks glass
  2. Combine all ingredients except lemon peel in stirring glass
  3. Fill stirring glass with ice and let sit
  4. While cocktail is diluting, take chilled rocks glass and add the barspoon of absinthe. Rinse the glass by tilting it and allowing the absinthe to coat the entire glass. Dump remaining absinthe once complete
  5. Check dilution of cocktail in stirring glass. Stir longer if necessary
  6. Strain cocktail into absinthe rinsed glass
  7. Express lemon peel over cocktail and garnish the side of the glass with it
  8. Enjoy!
